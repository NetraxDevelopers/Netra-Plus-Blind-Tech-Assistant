Accessibility Statement

1. Commitment to Accessibility

At Netra Plus – Smart Blind Tech Assistant, our absolute foundational priority is digital inclusion. As an advanced Blind Accessibility App, we are passionately committed to ensuring our platform and website are accessible to everyone, including individuals with low vision, complete blindness, and other forms of disabilities. We continually uphold strict adherence to the WCAG 2.1 principles and push the boundaries of equal digital access.

2. Screen Reader Support

Because we serve the blind community, every layer of our platform, from our Android Accessibility App interfaces to this very website, is fundamentally engineered to function natively and seamlessly with industry-standard screen readers like TalkBack, Jaws, and NVDA. ARIA labeling and logical heading structures are deployed religiously to provide complete conversational navigation context.

3. Voice Navigation Support

Beyond screen reading, Netra Plus serves as an innovative AI Assistant App by integrating extensive Voice Navigation capabilities. Users can command actions, trigger our built-in OCR Reader App, and maneuver through critical components entirely through voice, enabling completely hands-free assistive tooling.

4. High Contrast Mode

For our users with low vision or macular sensitivities, we guarantee strict high-contrast compliance. The app incorporates customizable deep-dark themes and extremely readable font systems designed explicitly to reduce eye strain, glare blindness, and contrast distortion, while utilizing safe color methodologies.

5. Keyboard Navigation

Every digital touchpoint created by Netra X Developers is explicitly architected to operate efficiently without a mouse. Tab targeting, visual focus indicators, and strict sequence ordering exist to provide effortless keyboard navigation.

6. Reduced Motion Support

Inclusive design requires flexibility. Netra Plus correctly responds to system-level `prefers-reduced-motion` settings. If a user dictates reduced animation, all dynamic fades, sliding scales, and high-velocity transitions are securely disabled to prevent vestibular disorders or distraction.

7. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is not a feature for us; it is our entire company mission. We are engaged in continuous, lifelong iterations of our Object Detection App tools. We run exhaustive beta tests strictly with impaired communities to capture genuine feedback and to perpetually refine our assistive offerings.

8. Feedback and Accessibility Support Contact

If you encounter any barriers to entry, experience software friction, or simply want to demand more accessible performance from any of our features, we are listening aggressively. Please reach out to us at any time:
