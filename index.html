About Our Mission

Netra Plus is built with one core purpose: to help blind and visually impaired individuals use smartphones with total independence. By focusing heavily on digital accessibility, we ensure no one is left behind in the tech revolution.

Through our advanced AI-powered tools, users can seamlessly navigate apps, understand their environment, read documents, and stay safe. Netra Plus gives you the digital independence you deserve.
