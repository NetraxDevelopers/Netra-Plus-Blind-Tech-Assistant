1. Introduction

Welcome to Netra Plus – Smart Blind Tech Assistant. Netra X Developers ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of all our users. As a Blind Accessibility App, we take data ethics strictly and aim to provide transparent information on how we collect, use, and protect your data. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices to comply with Google Play Developer Policy and other global standards like GDPR and Indian IT laws.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with a powerful AI Assistant App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We may collect your Name and Email Address when you explicitly provide them through contact forms or feedback.
  • Usage Data: We track anonymous interactions within the app to improve user experience.
  • Device Information: We collect device models, operating system versions, and crash reports to ensure stability.
  • Accessibility Settings Usage: We analyze the activation of certain accessibility features strictly to optimize our OCR Reader App and Object Detection App components.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, maintain, and dramatically improve the core functionalities of the Android Accessibility App.
  • Respond seamlessly to your customer support queries via netraplushelpdesk@gmail.com.
  • Ensure safety, prevent fraud, and quickly resolve software bugs.

4. Third-Party Services

Netra Plus integrates with proven third-party platforms to enhance functionality:

  • Google Play Services: Required for secure app delivery and updates.
  • Firebase: Used for real-time database management and crash analytics securely.
  • Analytics Tools: Employed to gather aggregated user data, completely stripped of personally identifiable information.

5. Data Security

We deploy rigorous administrative, technical, and physical security measures to guard your personal information. Despite our intensive efforts, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application is not explicitly directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we take steps to securely delete that information immediately.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as is strictly necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, and to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations.

8. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, rectify, or request the erasure of any personal data we hold about you. Contact us directly to exercise these rights.
